About

The 3D geospatial vision research group is part of the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics, Faculty of Construction and Environment of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. It concerns itself with applying 3D remote sensing and computer vision methods to solve practical and theoretical problems in the field of urban and . These systems help in environmental modelling, crisis management, automate, information modelling, modelling of the interior of buildings, etc.

It is a multidisciplinary group (computer scientists, geomatics engineers, and geographers) composed of 1 Faculty member and several PhD students, postdocs and visitors.
