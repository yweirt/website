Our group presents 10 papers at the 3D Athens 2016 conference
Oct 6, 2016

This year’s 3D Geoinfo 2016 conference is organised as a part of the Joint 3D Athens Conference 2016. The event comprises two other 3D events: Indoor 3D Workshop and the 5th International FIG Workshop on 3D Cadastres.

The 3D Geoinformation group at TU Delft is represented with 10 papers (Open Access):


First 3D Cadastral Registration of Multi-level Ownerships Rights in the Netherlands
J. Stoter, H. Ploeger, R. Roes, E. van der Riet, F. Biljecki, and H. Ledoux
5th International FIG Workshop on 3D Cadastres, 491-504, 2016.


Proposal for a new LOD and multi-representation concept for CityGML
M.-O. Löwner, G. Gröger, J. Benner, F. Biljecki, and C. Nagel
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 3-12, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-3-2016


The most common geometric and semantic errors in CityGML datasets
F. Biljecki, H. Ledoux, X. Du, J. Stoter, K. H. Soon, and V. H. S. Khoo
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 13-22, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-13-2016


Towards automatic semantic labelling of 3D city models
M. Rook, F. Biljecki, and A. A. Diakité
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 23-30, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-23-2016


A CityGML extension for handling very large TINs
K. Kumar, H. Ledoux, and J. Stoter
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 137-143, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-137-2016


Defining simple nD operations based on prismatic nD objects
K. Arroyo Ohori, H. Ledoux, and J. Stoter
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 155-162, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-155-2016


Extraction of the 3D free space from building models for indoor navigation
A. A. Diakité and S. Zlatanova
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 241-248, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-241-2016


Indoor A* pathfinding through an octree representation of a point cloud
O. B. P. M. Rodenberg, E. Verbree, and S. Zlatanova
Int. Arch. Photogramm. Remote Sens. Spatial Inf. Sci., IV-2-W1: 249-255, 2016.
doi:10.5194/isprs-annals-IV-2-W1-249-2016


LADM and IndoorGML for support of indoor space identification
