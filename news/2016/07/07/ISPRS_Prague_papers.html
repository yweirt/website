The 3D Geoinformation team at the ISPRS Congress 2016 in Prague
Jul 7, 2016

From the 12th to the 19th of July, will be held in Prague the 23rd ISPRS Congress. That event being a big rendez-vous for several GIS actors in the world, the 3D Geoinformation group at TU Delft greatly marks its presence with a total of 14 papers in which the team’s members are involved. Jantien, Sisi, Kavisha, and Abdou will be there to present some of the papers, which are already available online and detailed below.
