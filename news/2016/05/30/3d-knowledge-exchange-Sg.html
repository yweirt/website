3D knowledge exchange between TU Delft and Singapore
May 30, 2016

On Friday 27 May we have organised a workshop at TU Delft to discuss experiences and collaboration in 3D geoinformation between the Netherlands and Singapore. We have welcomed representatives of four agencies of the Singapore Government: Singapore Land Authority (SLA), Infocomm Development Authority of Singapore (iDA), Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA), and the National Research Foundation (NRF).

Experts from several Dutch institutions and companies were present as well to convey their expertise and current work: City of Amsterdam, City of the Hague, City of Rotterdam, Tygron, ESRI, Future Insight, and Kadaster.

During this insightful knowledge exchange we have heard about interesting work of all participants. Our ongoing collaboration with Singaporean agencies already resulted in a few visits.
